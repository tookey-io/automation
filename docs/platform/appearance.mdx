<Tip>
You can set up a unique domain for your platform, like app.yoursaas.com. This option is for the cloud edition, and you need to get in touch with sales@activepieces.com to activate it.
</Tip>

To tweak how your platform looks, head to the **Appearance** section under **Settings**. Here, you can customize:
- Logo
- Logo Icon
- Favicon
- Primary color
